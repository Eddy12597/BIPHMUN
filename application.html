Academic and Conference Code of Conduct

BIPH Model United Nations is an educational simulation for developing research, debate, and public speaking skills. All participants are expected to maintain professional, respectful, and academically focused communications throughout the conference.

Participants are expected to:

- Engage in discussions for academic and educational purposes only

- Maintain a professional, respectful, and fact-based tone in all conference-related communications

- Focus on structured debate, resolution drafting, and policy simulation.

Informal or non-academic discussions are outside the scope of the conference.

Scope of Discussion / Safe Topics

Delegates should focus on:

- Resolution drafting, debate, and procedural discussions

- Academic reasoning, research, and logical argumentation

Delegates should AVOID:

- References to real-world political conflicts, current events, or religious groups that are NOT RELATED to the listed topics

- Topics that could be interpreted as sensitive, provocative, or extreme

Discussions that are unrelated to the educational objectives of the conference

You are encouraged to use creativity in simulations while keeping all discussion professional and academically focused.

学术及会议行为准则

BIPH模拟联合国大会旨在培养研究、辩论和公共演讲能力，是一项教育模拟活动。所有参与者均应在整个会议期间保持专业、尊重且以学术为中心的沟通方式。

参与者应：

- 仅参与学术和教育目的的讨论

- 在所有与会议相关的沟通中保持专业、尊重且基于事实的语气

- 专注于结构化辩论、决议起草和政策模拟

非正式或非学术性讨论不属于会议范围。

讨论范围 / 安全话题

代表应重点关注：

- 学术推理、研究和逻辑论证

- 决议草案起草、辩论和程序性讨论

代表应避免：

- 提及与所列主题无关的现实世界政治冲突、时事或宗教团体

- 可能被解读为敏感、挑衅或极端的话题

- 人身攻击或与会议教育目标无关的讨论

鼓励您在模拟环节发挥创造力，但请保持所有讨论的专业性和学术性。
