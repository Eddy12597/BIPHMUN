About BIPHMUN
__________

BIPHMUN was established in 2022 by Fiona Chiang, Aaron Zhang, and Micky Yang. Since its inception, it has grown into a vibrant platform for students to engage in meaningful debate, explore global issues, and refine their diplomatic skills. The conference brings together delegates from diverse backgrounds, fostering collaboration, critical thinking, and innovative solutions to the challenges facing our world today. Whether participating in intense committee sessions or engaging in cross-cultural exchanges, delegates leave BIPHMUN with not only enhanced knowledge, but also lifelong friendships and invaluable leadership experience.
