checklist for changing/adding committees, or updating each year

0. Change the homepage:
    a. index.html: year and theme
    b. invitation letter under that
    c. (if applicable) the group photo at the bottom of index.html
1. Change the bus schedule photo in accommodation.html and the text, if applicable
2. Change the conference info in application.html and update the links
3. Change the committees:
    a. Usually five committees are enough. If you are going from 4 to 5, uncomment the bottom one. If you are going from 5 to 6 (congrats!), copy and paste the fourth at the end. Skip this if you are not adding/removing committees. Please comment the html if you are removing committees
    b. Change the src and alt attributes of the <image> tag
    c. Change the committee title
    d. Change the committee topics
    e. Change the committee-desc-title
    f. Change the committee level, if applicable
    g. Change the chairs
    h. Change the BG href and download attributes (also the years) for the <a> tag
4. Change the conference schedule and bus schedule in schedule.html
5. Change title years
6. Update timeline in <about> (and their css)
