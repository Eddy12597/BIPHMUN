BIPHMUN Conference 2026

Confronting Contemporary Challenges: Climate, Crypto, Commerce, and Conflict

ABOUT BIPHMUN

We cordially invite you and your school to attend the fifth BASIS International School Park Lane Harbour Model United Nations that will take place on March 20-22 at our campus in Huizhou.

This is our fifth annual conference, and with the success from last year, we hope to deliver the same levels of professionalism, integrity, and hospitality. Our aim is to provide an opportunity for students to demonstrate their academic excellence, to improve on personal skills such as public speaking and critical thinking, as well as to foster a MUN community of global citizens to discuss international issues.

With the dawn of the digital era where technology has become integral to diplomatic efforts, the priority is to balance power, responsibility, and ethics. Recognizing the urgency of this matter, BIPHMUN 2026 endeavors to delve into the resolution of these pressing issues. Its overarching objective is to illuminate the present state of these challenges with the advancement of technology, preventing the escalation of these issues out of malice. It is within this framework that we extend a warm invitation to participate in BIPHMUN 2026, providing a platform to explore, analyze, and debate the impending challenges in this decade, as encapsulated by our theme: Confronting Contemporary Challenges: Climate, Crypto, Commerce, and Conflict.
