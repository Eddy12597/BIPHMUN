BIPHMUN Conference 2026

Confronting Contemporary Challenges: Climate, Crypto, Commerce, and Conflict

ABOUT BIPHMUN

We cordially invite you and your school to attend the fifth BASIS International School Park Lane Harbour Model United Nations that will take place on March 20-22, 2026 at our campus in Huizhou.

This is our fifth annual conference, and with the success from last year, we hope to deliver the same levels of professionalism, integrity, and hospitality. Our aim is to provide an opportunity for students to demonstrate their academic excellence, to improve on personal skills such as public speaking and critical thinking, as well as to foster a MUN community of global citizens to discuss international issues.

In an era defined by rapid global change, today’s most pressing challenges increasingly intersect across climate sustainability, digital finance, international trade, and geopolitical conflict. BIPHMUN 2026 is structured around these interconnected issues, encouraging delegates to critically examine how climate policy, cryptocurrency and emerging financial systems, global commerce, and security dynamics shape the modern international order. Through rigorous debate and collaboration, participants will engage with real-world dilemmas faced by policymakers, developing informed, ethical, and forward-looking solutions. It is within this context that we invite students to join BIPHMUN 2026—a platform designed to foster analytical depth, diplomatic skill, and global awareness—under our theme: Confronting Contemporary Challenges: Climate, Crypto, Commerce, and Conflict.
